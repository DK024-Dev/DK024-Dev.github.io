WSAC

WSAC aims to create a platform for buying, selling, and swapping cars in a unique and efficient way. It goes beyond traditional classifieds by adding interactive features and fostering a community feel.
Project Date February 1st, 2023 to January 15th, 2024
Role Flutter Developer, Application Developer
Client Jamie Kingsley
Visit weswapanycar.com

Features:

  • Swapping System: Users can list their cars for swapping and browse through other available vehicles, searching by specific criteria or browsing curated selections. This incentivizes upgrades or changes without incurring significant expense.
  • Enhanced Listings: Instead of static text and photos, listings can include video workarounds, virtual tours, and performance data reports, giving buyers a more immersive experience.
  • Community Hub: Create a forum or chat space for users to discuss cars, share expertise, organize repair workshops, and find other car enthusiasts in their area.
  • Valuation Tool: Integrate an AI-powered car valuation tool to help users determine fair market prices for buying, selling, and swapping.
  • Escrow Service: Offer an optional escrow service for secure transactions, protecting both buyers and sellers from fraud.

    • Benefits:

  • Simplifies the car buying/selling process: Provides a platform for efficient transactions and removes the hassle of traditional methods.
  • Promotes sustainability: Encourages car reuse and reduces the environmental impact of vehicle production and disposal.
  • Builds a community: Creates a space for car enthusiasts to connect, share knowledge, and support each other.
  • Offers unique value: Swapping system stands out from traditional platforms and potentially attracts a wider audience.


    • Target Audience:

  • Car enthusiasts of all ages and levels of expertise.
  • Individuals looking to upgrade their car without a significant financial burden.
  • Environmentally conscious consumers seeking sustainable car ownership options.

    • Monetization:

  • Charge a listing fee for cars, with premium options for enhanced visibility.
  • Implement a commission-based system for successful swaps and sales facilitated through the platform.
  • Partner with relevant businesses for sponsored content and advertising opportunities.
