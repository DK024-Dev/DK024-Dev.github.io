    Project Description

    Equiti Gold reimagines physical gold investment by packaging it into a modern, user-friendly mobile experience. Available on both iOS and Android, the platform lets users buy, sell, gift, and even receive physical gold deliveries—all within a few taps.

    Upon downloading and opening the app, investors can create a free account using simple identity documents (UAE ID or passport), email, and a selfie for security. After linking payment methods—such as bank transfers or credit/debit cards—they can begin purchasing gold in increments as small as 1 gram, lowering the entry barrier to precious metal investment.

    Beyond buying and selling, Equiti Gold allows you to gift gold instantly to other app users via a QR code and supports secure physical deliveries. Physical orders become available when holdings reach 50 grams or more, with insured courier service and certified packaging provided. The recipient must provide ID or passport upon delivery—ensuring security throughout the process.

    In summary, Equiti Gold modernizes gold ownership through a clean and transparent digital platform that combines accessibility, flexibility, and peace of mind. Whether you’re a beginner wanting to dip your toes into precious metals or a seasoned investor looking for a secure hedge, the app’s features—live pricing, minimal investment size, gifting, delivery, and regulation—position it as a compelling solution. It's a seamless path to diversify your portfolio, combat inflation, and stay connected to one of the world’s oldest and most reliable assets.
